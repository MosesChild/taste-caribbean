A Taste of the Carribean

Since 2009, Taste of Caribbean Island has been catering to Vancouverites who delight in the exotic and comforting flavours of the West Indies.

From Casual to elegant, Chef Marquise Vilmont prepares the finest organic ingredients combining culinary skill and classic Caribbean Cuisine.

Re-live your favorite paradise dishes. and discover new ones. We can help you plan your unique event to ensure you and your guests have a wonderful, memorable occasion.

A Word from Chef Vilmont

When people think of Caribbean they think of sunshine, vacation and fun. It is also known for very tasty and healthy cuisine. But what makes our cuisine so different?​
​
The Caribbean cuisine is delicious because we have our own way to use and blend spices when we marinate the meat and when we cook. ​
​
We clean our meat with lemon, lime, sour oranges juices, vinegar before preparation. Then the meat is rinsed.​
​
Caribbean cuisine is very healthy. We marinate all our meat making it easy to digest and use lots of fresh veggies and fresh fruits.​
This way we keep ourselves and our customers healthy....and happy. ​

This is why Caribbean cuisine is so different, tasty and healthy. When you taste it you know it's Caribbean cuisine. ​
To taste it is to love it! ​
We look forward to serve you!

Chef Marquise Vilmont was brought up in Martinique. There, she grew up loving to cook and attended Anne-Marie Chevalier in the Carribean Islands to become a chef... Since becoming licensed at Vancouver Community College in 2009, her restaurant and catering has been making the locals rave about her sumptuous Caribbean Cuisine.

Catering

We specialize in catering with a Caribbean flair. From casual to formal, you bring the people and we bring the food. From appetizers and refreshments, to buffets and full multi-course meals, let our food help create the special occasion you will remember... And don’t forget dessert!

Event Planning

No matter what the occasion, we can help create your memorable event. We offer complete party planning, including tents, seating, entertainment, food, and drinks. Planning a Sweet 16 Anniversary or Graduation party? We've got you covered. Elegant or Casual, whatever style you like. Work with our seasoned event designers to plan the perfect event.
